# Literature & Psychology Guide

**How great literature teaches us about human psychology, culture, and the human condition.**

This repository is a collection of essays exploring the deep connections between literature and the human mind. From Dostoevsky's tortured criminals to Austen's sharp social observers, the greatest novels ever written are also some of the most insightful studies of human psychology ever produced.

Whether you're a student, a lifelong reader, or someone looking to understand yourself and others more deeply, these essays offer a starting point for reading literature with new eyes.
